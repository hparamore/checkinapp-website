Features

Less planning, more hanging out

Checkin replaces the "anyone free?" group text with something way simpler. Here's how it works.

🔥

Light Your Fire

Signal that you're available, arriving soon, or planning to be on later. Your campfire crew sees your status instantly.

👥

Campfire Groups

Create campfires for your friend groups, gaming squads, or anyone you want to coordinate with. Share a code to invite people in.

🌍

Time Zone Aware

Checkin shows everyone's local time so you can see when your friends across the world are actually free.

🎲

Campfire Tools

Roll dice, create polls, track counters, and more. Built-in tools to help your group decide what to do once everyone's together.

🔔

Smart Notifications

Get notified when friends check in so you never miss a session. Customize alerts per group to keep things quiet when you want.

Discord Integration

Link your Discord and your game activity shows up automatically in Checkin, so friends know exactly what you're playing.

Hunter Paramore

Designer & Developer of Checkin

My friends and I kept hitting the same wall. We all wanted to play together, but figuring out who's free across different time zones through a group chat was way more effort than it should be. So I built something simpler. You just say when you're going to be on, and everyone else can see that and decide if they can join.

I've been designing apps for over 12 years, and Checkin is my first real swing at shipping one myself. I build and maintain it in my free time. It's a labor of love, and I'm just getting started.

If you have ideas, feedback, or just want to say hi, come find me on the Checkin Discord.
I read everything, and would love to chat!

Checkin is free and built by one person. Your support helps keep the servers running, new features coming, and makes it possible for me to keep working on this. Every bit helps.
